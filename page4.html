She's the First at Stony Brook

We are the independent Stony Brook chapter of She’s the First, a not-for profit
organization that sponsors girls’ education in low-income countries.

What we do at Stony Brook?

Our goal is to raise awareness about the challenges surrounding young girls across the world who are striving to be the first in their families to graduate high school and to raise money to provide scholarships to girls in low-income countries.

How can you get involved?

We host various events throughout the semester including fundraisers, awareness workshops and more. Please feel free to stop by. Learn more >
